How to use the palace

The art of memory, briefly

The method of loci — the memory palace — pins each item you must remember to a place along a route you know well. To recall the list, you walk the route in your mind and the places hand the items back, in order.

Journey guides you along the route with a compass; stand at each glowing locus to meet its image. Recall darkens every locus and asks you to type what lived there. List lets you move your own items in — one per line — and the palace re-shelves itself.
